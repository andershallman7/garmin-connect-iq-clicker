# Privacy Policy for IP Counter

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

## Overview

IP Counter ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in relation to the App.

## Data Collection

The IP Counter app:
- **Does NOT collect** any personal information
- **Does NOT collect** location data
- **Does NOT collect** health or fitness data
- **Does NOT transmit** any data to external servers
- **Does NOT require** internet connectivity

The App operates entirely on your Garmin device and stores data locally only.

## Data Storage

- All counter data is stored locally on your Garmin device only
- No data is synchronized to cloud services
- No data is shared with third parties

## Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function:
- Basic watchface/app display functionality

## User Rights

You have complete control over the App:
- You may uninstall the App at any time
- All data is automatically deleted when the App is uninstalled
- You can reset the counter to zero at any time

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be noted by the "Last Updated" date above.
